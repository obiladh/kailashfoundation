Our Team

Bashu Kumar Adhikari

Executive Director

Beyond his professional commitments, our director is a compassionate individual and a strong advocate for helping the needy. Actively engaged in social work throughout various locations in Nepal, he contributes to making a positive impact in the lives of those who require assistance.

Obil Adhikari

Program Director

His personal commitment to exploring with minimal environmental impact and engaging local communities reflects a vision for a sustainable and vibrant future for these breathtaking regions. In the world of Mr. Adhikari, travel is a force for positive change, leaving behind a legacy of preserved beauty and empowered communities.

Eliza Bhatta

Finance Manager

Eliza Bhatta embodies compassion and care for the needy. Her journey is marked by a steadfast dedication to uplifting others through tangible acts of kindness. From volunteering to founding initiatives, Eliza's mission is to make a meaningful difference in the lives of the less fortunate. Join her in creating a world where empathy and support are the pillars of society.

Sujan Bhatta

Program Manager

Sujan Bhatta leads treks through the Himalayas, conquering peaks and hearts alike. His adventures are fueled by a deep commitment to his community. Through sustainable practices and community development initiatives, he ensures that his journeys leave a positive impact on both the environment and the people who call the mountains home. Join Sujan as he blazes trails and gives back to the land and its inhabitants.

Ram Gopal Adhikari

Community Outreach Coordinator

Ram Gopal Adhikari is a seasoned trek guide specializing in Kailash tours and Nepal treks. Beyond his expertise in mountain adventures, he's deeply committed to social work. With a passion for both exploration and community development, Ram Gopal ensures every journey leaves a lasting positive impact. Join him for an adventure that goes beyond the trails.

Syangbu Lama

Advocacy Specialist

Sangbu Lama, Ward Chairman in Sindhupalchok, Nepal, is dedicated to community development and improving livelihoods. Through his leadership, he spearheads initiatives that empower residents and foster a prosperous future for all. Join him in building a better tomorrow for Sindhupalchok.

Sanu Sherpa

Volunteer Coordinator

Sanu Sherpa is a local community leader and passionate advocate for social welfare. Through her advocacy and leadership, she works tirelessly to uplift her community and create positive change.
