A Part of The Kailash Foundation

The Nandi relentlessly volunteers his life to the service of Lord Shiva. Nandi, the sacred bull, holds a revered place in Hindu mythology and culture. As the faithful companion of Lord Shiva, Nandi symbolizes strength, loyalty, and devotion. Beyond his symbolic significance, Nandi represents the inseparable bond between humans and animals, embodying the harmony and interconnectedness of all living beings.
